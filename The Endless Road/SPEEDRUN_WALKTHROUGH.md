## 🎯 Key Strategy

| Phase | Why | Benefit |
|-------|-----|---------|
| **Talisman First** | Get protection before night | Completely safe during nights |
| **Safehouse Setup** | Hang at house early | All nights are safe (search filler) |
| **Never Invite NPCs** | Wastes moves + traitor risk | Saves 2 moves, avoid betrayal |
| **Cave Efficiency** | Visit twice (1 item each) | Never trigger monster wake-up |
| **Radio Code: 4275** | Diary pages fixed | Diary 1=4, 2=2, 3=7, 4=5 |

**What You Avoid**:
- ❌ Talking to NPCs (no mechanical benefit)
- ❌ Exploring diner, church, sheriff, etc. (time wasted)
- ❌ Health items (take minimal RNG damage)
- ❌ Inviting anyone (traitor risk)
