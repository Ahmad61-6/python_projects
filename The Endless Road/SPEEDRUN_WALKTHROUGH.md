## 💡 Key Strategy Notes

### Why This Route Works

1. **Talisman First**: Sets up immediate protection. This prevents any night-time damage.

2. **Explore Safe**: Once safehouse is set up, you're invulnerable to night attacks. Can take your time gathering parts.

3. **Cave Efficiency**: You visit the cave twice:
   - Visit 1: Grab battery pack (1 of 3 turns used)
   - Visit 2: Grab signal booster (1 of 3 new turns used)
   - You NEVER grab 3+ items, so no monster wake-up

4. **Diary Collection**: By searching during night (safe), you eventually find all diary pages. This is implicit in the speedrun (search enough times, you discover all items).

5. **Radio Code is Fixed**: Regardless of which NPC is the Traitor or which dialogue you see, the code is always `4275`. No guessing required.

### Optimal Moments

- **Longest Safe Window**: Night time inside safehouse. Can do anything except move locations.
- **Shortest Danger**: Forest during day (small RNG damage possible, but acceptable).
- **No Traitor Risk**: You never invite any NPC, so the Traitor can't betray you.

### What You Avoid

- **Inviting NPCs**: Wastes 1 move, risks traitor betrayal. Skip entirely.
- **Exploring Other Locations**: Diner, Church, Sheriff, etc. ignored to save moves.
- **Using Health Items**: You don't take enough damage to need them.
- **Talking to NPCs**: Wastes time, no mechanical benefit for speedrun.
